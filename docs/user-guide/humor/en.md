# Three situations when ARGUS won't help you

> Honest limits from a self-aware agent

ARGUS is a personal AI agent on your machine — not a genie, not your lawyer, and definitely not the friend who says "yeah, disable the firewall, what could go wrong." It has WARDEN (a security firewall for MCP tools), hard budget caps, and a stubborn refusal to become an accomplice.

This page is unofficial, lightly self-deprecating, and probably more honest than most landing pages. If you recognize yourself below — good. That means you're human.

## 1. You asked it to bypass WARDEN and "just run the tool"

You found an MCP server in a Discord thread called `totally-legit-wallet-drainer-v2`. You pasted the URL and said "don't scan it, I trust the dev." WARDEN scanned it anyway. Reputation: rock bottom. Severity: high. Your agent didn't freeze — it just said no, the way a good bouncer does, without debating your life choices.

## 2. It's 3:17 a.m. and you're emotionally dumping on a budget-capped CLI

You opened `argus chat` to ask whether existence has meaning, whether your ex was "a lesson," and whether you should text them. ARGUS will answer kindly — briefly — then remind you it has a token budget, not a therapy license. It won't sit through forty-seven messages of spiral. That's a feature, not cruelty. Go drink water.

## 3. Your task needs a PhD thesis; your budget buys a sticky note

You asked for "full competitive analysis of every AI agent marketplace since 2019, with citations, SWOT, and a pitch deck." ARGUS started, hit `maxUsdPerTask`, and stopped — by design. It won't bankrupt you chasing perfection. Give it a scoped task: five competitors, one table, done. Agents that never stop billing aren't smart; they're rude.

## When ARGUS actually shows up

Clear tasks with a finish line: refactor a file, summarize a PDF, debug a 502, research five vendors in a table. Legitimate MCP tools that pass WARDEN. Questions in any language. It finishes the job, reports what it did, and stops — like a contractor who leaves when the work is done instead of inventing new rooms to renovate.
